# ProteinMPNN

A message-passing neural network for protein sequence design that predicts amino acid sequences likely to fold into a given backbone structure.

## Role in the RFdiffusion3 pipeline

[[rfdiffusion3]] generates all-atom structures but does not output sequences. ProteinMPNN is used downstream to assign sequences to the generated backbones in protein-only contexts. For designs involving non-protein atoms (small molecules, DNA), [[ligandmpnn]] is used instead.

In the RFD3 paper, 8 sequences per backbone are typically generated by ProteinMPNN, then evaluated by [[alphafold3]] for predicted fold accuracy.
