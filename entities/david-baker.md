# David Baker

Professor at the University of Washington and director of the Institute for Protein Design (IPD); Howard Hughes Medical Institute investigator; senior and corresponding author on the RFdiffusion family of models.

## Role in this wiki

Baker's lab developed [[rfdiffusion]], [[rfdiffusion2]], and [[rfdiffusion3]], as well as key downstream tools like [[proteinmpnn]] and Rosetta. He is the corresponding author on the RFdiffusion3 paper alongside Rohith Krishna.
